About us

Mr Bala got in touch with some young girls who did not complete their academics, and then he collaborated with corporate hospitals that were ready to provide skill-based training. Close to 30 women have found jobs as lab technicians in nearby cities where the pay is almost triple the meagre amount that they were given for making beedis. With a future perspective, Mr Bala decided to empower youth by gathering them from his nearby villages to train them with employment skills with the help of his friends and few other vocational training centres. Initially, it was a challenging task for Mr Bala to gather youth who were wearing tattered clothes, hanging up in drinking, and engaging in street fights. Who now have transformed into well dressed in formals and well settled with good salary package.
